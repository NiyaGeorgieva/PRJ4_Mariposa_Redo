Classes

Hiphop

Urban dance characterized by four elements: Rap (Oral: recite or sing), "DJing" (Auditory or musical), Breaking (Physical: dance) and Graffiti (visual: painting).

Zumba

Dance discipline of Colombian origin in which different styles of music are mixed. Very energetic movements. A dose of joy and dynamism.

Ballet

Ballet or classical dance is the basis of all dance styles. It requires concentration, discipline and effort to achieve precise, coordinated and harmonic movements.

Salsa

Salsa is a syncretic dance form with origins from Cuba. It is normally a partner dance, although there are recognized solo forms such as solo dancing!

Breakdance

Break dancing, also called breaking and B-boying, energetic form of dance, fashioned and popularized by African Americans and U.S. Latinos, that includes stylized footwork and athletic moves such as back spins or head spins.

Show-Dance

A totally different kind of dance. We dance all the styles you can imagine: disco, cabaret, tango, burlesque ... Forget your complexes and get excited dancing. A weekly dose of energy, good vibes, vitality and smiles!

Commercial-dance

Most recent style of the new school "new styles" that integrates elements of all styles of current urban dances (hip hop, vogueing, dancehall, ...).
